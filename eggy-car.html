About Eggy Car

Eggy Car is a fun and challenging game where you drive a car with an egg on top over hilly roads. The goal is to drive as far as possible without letting the egg fall off the car.
You can collect coins to unlock new cars and power-ups to help you on your journey.
Grab the freeze power-up to keep the egg in place for a few seconds.
Find the right balance between speed and stability to keep the egg from falling off.

Controls

Use the left and right arrow keys, or the A and D keys, to move the car.
